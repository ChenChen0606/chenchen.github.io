MR. & MS. TCGC

2023-2024

Attending the Mr. and Miss TCGC 2024 was an unforgettable experience. I went there to support someone I know, thinking it might be boring since it was my first time, but I enjoyed the event so much. The energy of the crowd was amazing, and I couldn’t help but join in, cheering and clapping along with everyone. It was exciting and fun, and I’ll always remember it.
